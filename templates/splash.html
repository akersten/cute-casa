CuteCasa

Centralized home administration.

Login

IoT Integration

Connect your devices using Scrymg and command them online. Aggregate and analyze data throughout your home.

Floorplans

Use our innovative floorplan editor to draw your house. Control devices in every room.

Task Delegation

Keep track of who needs to take out the garbage, do the dishes, sweep the floors, and clean the bathroom. Automatically generate fair schedules and send text reminders.

Dashboards

See the heartbeat of your house — view data from all modules in one place to see what needs doing.

Maintenance Reminders

Set up SMS alerts and never forget to change a furnace filter again. We'll walk you through setting up only the reminders that matter to your house.

Lease Management

Set up automatic payments via Stripe integration — never miss rent again!

Contact
